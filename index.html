Introduction

We visualize racial differences in intergenerational wealth mobility based on data from the Panel Study of Income Dynamics (see Pfeffer/Killewald 2018). The display of intergenerational mobility between parental net worth (in the 1980s) and their adult children's net worth (in 2013/2015) focuses, first, on racial differences in mobility rates and, second, on the maintenance of the racial wealth structure.

Visualization 1: Mobility Rates

displays wealth mobility rates for white and black children and reveals the higher likelihood of downward wealth mobility for black children.

Visualization 2: Wealth Structure

shows that thedespite intergenerational fluctuation in wealth, racial gaps in wealth are largely maintained across generations.
